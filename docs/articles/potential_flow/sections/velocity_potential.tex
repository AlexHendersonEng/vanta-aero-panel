\section{Velocity Potential}
\label{sec:velocity-potential}

For irrotational flow a scalar potential exists such that
\[
\mathbf{V}=\nabla \phi
\]

The velocity components become
\[
u=\frac{\partial \phi}{\partial x},
\qquad
v=\frac{\partial \phi}{\partial y}
\]

Substitution into continuity yields
\[
\nabla^2 \phi =0
\]

Therefore, the velocity potential satisfies Laplace's equation.
